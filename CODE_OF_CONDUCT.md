# Code of Conduct

This project adopts the **Contributor Covenant 2.1** as its code of conduct.

Full text: https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/

## Summary

We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free
experience for everyone, regardless of background, experience level, gender,
gender identity and expression, sexual orientation, disability, personal
appearance, body size, race, ethnicity, age, religion, or nationality.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be
reported privately to the project maintainers via
[GitHub](https://github.com/anemoi-ai/helix) (direct message or a private
report). All
complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

Maintainers are obligated to respect the privacy and security of the reporter.
